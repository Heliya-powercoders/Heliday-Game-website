Number Guessing Game

I'm thinking of a number between 1 and 100. Can you guess it?

Game Rules

1️⃣ The computer picks a random number between 1 and 100.
2️⃣ Type your guess in the box and click “Guess”.
3️⃣ You'll get hints: “Too high” or “Too low.”
4️⃣ Keep guessing until you find the correct number
5️⃣ Click “Restart” anytime to play again.

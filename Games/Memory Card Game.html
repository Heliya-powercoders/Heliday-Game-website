Memory Card Game

Find matching sticker cards. Flip two cards to find identical stickers.

Moves: 0
Time: 00:00
Best: —
Tip: Click or press Enter/Space on a card to flip it. Matching identical symbols wins the pair.

Game Rules

1️⃣ Choose your grid size (Easy, Medium, or Hard).
2️⃣ Select a sticker set or shuffle stickers for variety.
3️⃣ Click “Start” to begin the game.
4️⃣ Flip two cards to find matching stickers.
5️⃣ Matched pairs stay flipped — find them all to win!
6️⃣ You can restart anytime with the “Restart” button.

Game Rules

1️⃣ Click “Add Word” and enter a word with its meaning.
2️⃣ Add as many pairs as you want to make the game more challenging.
3️⃣ Click “Start Game” to shuffle the cards and begin.
4️⃣ Click on two cards to find the matching word and meaning pair.
5️⃣ Matched pairs stay visible — find them all to win!
6️⃣ You can restart anytime using the “Restart” button.
